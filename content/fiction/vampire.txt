The engine purred to life with a sound like a satisfied predator. Olivia's hands tightened on the wheel—too fast, too hard—and the steering column groaned in protest. She loosened her grip with a hissed breath. Control. The word echoed Hassan's voice, but the hunger beneath it was all her own.
